Central AC


Rheem.1/06/2022

A central air conditioner is a type of air conditioning system that cools and dehumidifies the air in a home or building. It consists of an indoor unit and an outdoor unit connected by refrigerant lines. The system is controlled by a thermostat and uses refrigerant to cool and dehumidify the air.

Ductless/Mini-Split AC


Rheem.1/06/2022

A ductless or mini-split air conditioner is a type of air conditioning system that does not require ducts to distribute cooled air throughout a home or building. It consists of an outdoor unit, which contains the compressor and condenser, and one or more indoor units, which house the evaporator.

Floor Mounted


Rheem.1/06/2022

Floor mounted air conditioning units are a type of air conditioning system that is installed on the floor of a room or space. They typically have a rectangular or square shape and are designed to sit against a wall or in a corner. These units are self-contained, meaning that they have all of the necessary components contained within the unit.

Hybrib AC


Rheem.1/06/2022

Hybrid air conditioning systems, also known as dual fuel systems, combine an electric air conditioner with a natural gas or propane furnace. These systems use the electric air conditioner during the warmer months and the furnace during the colder months, providing year-round comfort.

Cassette AC


Rheem.1/06/2022

Cassette air conditioners are a type of air conditioning system that is designed to be installed in the ceiling of a room or space. They have a compact, square or rectangular shape and are typically installed in a suspended ceiling or an attic.

Portable AC


Rheem.1/06/2022

Portable air conditioners are a type of air conditioning system that can be easily moved from one room to another. They have a compact size and are typically designed with wheels for easy movement.
