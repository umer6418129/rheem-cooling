About Rheem

Rheem is a leading company in the air conditioning industry, known for its high-quality and energy-efficient air conditioning systems. With a history dating back to the 1920s, Rheem has established itself as a trusted and reliable brand in the HVAC market. The company offers a wide range of air conditioners, including central AC units, ductless mini-splits, and hybrid systems. Rheem is committed to sustainability and innovation, with a focus on developing products that are environmentally friendly and that help customers save energy and money.
